Welcome to the best place for your guitar setup needs! I provide a range of services so that you can really get the most out of your instrument.

I cover electric, acoustic and bass guitars. Here are a few of the most common jobs I do:

  • Full setup
  • Guitar re-stringing
  • Full fret dressing - level, crown and polish
  • Floating trem setup balancing - including vintage Fender style bridges
  • Acoustic guitar bridge and nut height adjustments

With two decades' worth of experience working with guitars of all shapes and sizes, you can rest assured that your pride and joy is in Safe Hands!

Guitars are fundamentally semi-organic - wood moves naturally from different factors. If you want to tune to C Standard with a set of 9-42s, you aren't going to have a good time! Consult with me to work out the needs for your new tuning, including string gauges, nut adjustment and bridge balancing. This can add a whole new character to your instrument!
